Who we are

Phlux was founded by Jonathan Fox, an engineer focused on bringing production-grade inference into real operational workflows. His background combines deep experience in flow assurance and industrial process systems with a strong foundation in physics-based simulation, project delivery, and business execution.

Delivery & leadership
