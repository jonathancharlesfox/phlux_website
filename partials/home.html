What we do

Phlux enables operators to extract more value from existing operational data by inferring key quantities that are difficult, costly, or impractical to measure directly. The emphasis is on scalable insight — solutions that can be validated on one asset and extended across similar systems without increasing field complexity.

Rather than introducing new instrumentation, Phlux leverages patterns in operational behavior to deliver reliable estimates of flow, system behavior, and equipment condition. This approach lowers deployment cost and enables solutions to scale across equipment with similar operating and physical characteristics.

What you’ll get

  • Clear scope and measurable outputs
  • Validation approach and acceptance criteria
  • Runtime packaging (schemas, preprocessing, versioning)
  • Monitoring plan (drift / reliability)

Value Drivers

By inferring key quantities from existing signals, Phlux avoids many of the cost and operational constraints associated with traditional hardware-heavy approaches.

Lower deployment friction

No need to purchase, install, or maintain additional field hardware. Inference is deployed in software, avoiding production shutdowns, hot work, and instrumentation lead times when adding or fixing existing equipment.

Scalability

Once validated on one asset, inference models can often be extended across equipment or lines with similar physics and operating characteristics — where traditional approaches would require separate physcial meters at each location.

Richer operational insights

Gain visibility into system behavior and performance, not just volumetric flow, through machine-learning inference applied to existing operational data.
